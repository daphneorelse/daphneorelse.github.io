Sample Bank

Four sample slots allow you to choose from system files or presets to get the perfect sound, and dial in the crop and gain. Then, switch between samples in-editor or on the fly by triggering them with MIDI.

Pattern Bank

With a bank of eight pattern slots, you can simply drag to reorder MIDI assignments, trigger playback, and access more utility functions.

Global Parameters

Three knobs control the global parameters: Gain (master volume), Release (amount of time after note-off to bring the saw back to origin), and Stretch (speed of pattern playback).

Pattern Editor

Two envelopes precisely control how the saw and fader move over time to create your scratch pattern. Auxillary controls allow you to load/save presets, toggle BPM sync, dial in the pattern length, enable snapping, and adjust the "momentum" smoothing of each envelope. In the editor, you can move nodes by dragging, selecting groups, resizing, or using special edit modes to create plateaus and easily generate 100% playback rate zones for drop scratches.
