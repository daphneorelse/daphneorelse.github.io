Take the tour

Oscillator

Built for synthesizing basic waveforms with complex gain and pitch envelopes. Design punishing basses, snappy kicks, and bold leads in seconds.

Noise

Use the gain and filter envelopes to morph white noise into extreme snares, clean claps, and eerie soundscapes. Explore different filter types and resonances for the perfect sound.

Delay

Choose between time-based or bpm-synced delay times, and turn on link mode to control left & right delay together. Adjust the built-in filter to add character, enabling you to create haunting echoes, harsh resonances, and everything in-between.

Reverb

Use the knobs or the visualization pad to scale the size of the reverberations, the stereo width of the effect, and the damping on high input frequencies. Add subtle room ambience or massive soundscapes for more intense explosive aftermath.

Filter

Dial in the cutoff and resonance of two filters, toggleable between lowpass/highpass and shelf modes. Boost low-end bass, cut unwanted high-end distortion, or add intense resonant peaks to your sound design.

Waveshaper

Take advantage of a powerful effects unit capable of adding subtle saturation, glitchy timbres, and crushing distortion. Regulate the input and output gain, as well as macro-controls that can transform your function horizontally and vertically.

LFO

Modulate almost any parameter in LaunchCodes with two LFOs, tunable to hertz or to host bpm. Just drag the crosshair to the desired control, and adjust the modulation range using CMD+drag.

Utilities

Use the big red button to create live MIDI and test out your sound design. Fine-tune the master gain and keep track of both RMS and peak levels with the stereo meter. Lastly, just hover over any control in LaunchCodes to read more about it in the description box.
